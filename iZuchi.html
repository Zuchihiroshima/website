ZUCHI HIROSHIMA

Nkosinathi Khumalo is a South African born photographer born and bred in Soweto. He completed his Advanced Programme in Photography at the Market Photo Workshop.
Nkosi uses the relation between the photographic process and the resulting image to speak about the contemporary landscape of South Africa. His work speaks about migration, industry and the displacement of Africa. He has also been featured in various group exhibition locally and internationally.
